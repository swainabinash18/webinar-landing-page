Frequently Asked Questions

Is this webinar only for PWNSAT students?

No, it's open to all students from Class 5 to 12—whether or not you’ve registered for PWNSAT.

How do I join the webinar?

It’s super easy! Just register with your name and number on this page, and you’ll receive a Zoom link via SMS and WhatsApp before the session. One click and you’re in—no extra app needed if you're on mobile or desktop.

What is PWNSAT and who can apply?

PWNSAT (Physics Wallah National Scholarship cum Admission Test) is for students in classes 6-12 and JEE/NEET droppers. It offers up to 100% scholarships on PW courses.

How do I stay connected after the webinar?

You can join our Telegram or WhatsApp channels for regular study tips, reminders, and bonus content.
